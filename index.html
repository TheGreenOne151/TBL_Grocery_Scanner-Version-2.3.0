📊 Data Sources & Certification References

Our scoring system is based on verified third-party certifications from these trusted organizations:

🏢

B Corp Certification

Certified B Corporations are businesses that meet the highest standards of verified social and environmental performance, public transparency, and legal accountability to balance profit and purpose.

🔗 Verify on B Corp Directory Score Impact: +1.0 to all pillars
⚖️

Fair Trade Certified

Fair Trade certification ensures that products are made according to rigorous social, environmental, and economic standards that support safe working conditions, environmental protection, and sustainable livelihoods for farmers and workers.

🔗 Verify on FLOCERT Score Impact: +1.0 social, +0.5 environmental, +0.5 economic
🌳

Rainforest Alliance Certified

The Rainforest Alliance certification seal means that the product was produced using methods that support the three pillars of sustainability: social, economic, and environmental, with a focus on protecting forests, improving livelihoods, and promoting human rights.

🔗 Verify on Rainforest Alliance Score Impact: +0.5 social, +1.0 environmental, +0.5 economic
🐰

Leaping Bunny Certified

The Leaping Bunny Program administers a cruelty-free standard and certification for companies producing cosmetic, personal care, and household products, ensuring no new animal testing is used in any phase of product development.

🔗 Verify on Leaping Bunny Score Impact: +1.0 social, +0.5 environmental

📈 Scoring Methodology

Base Score: All brands start with 5.0 in each pillar (Social, Environmental, Economic).

Certification Bonuses: Points are added only for verified third-party certifications.

Multi-Certification Bonus: Brands with multiple certifications receive an additional +0.5 to each pillar for each certification beyond the first.

Maximum Score: All scores are capped at 10.0 per pillar.
